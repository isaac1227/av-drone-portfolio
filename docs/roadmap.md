# Roadmap

## Version 1 - Design Foundation

- Home with hero, services and featured work.
- Portfolio with category selector.
- Services grouped by business type.
- About page with profile, experience, equipment and trust signals.
- Contact page with form and contact information.
- Base visual system aligned across the site.

## Phase 2 - CMS and Content

- Sanity integration for works and services.
- Dynamic portfolio and service management.
- Media workflow for cover images and galleries.
- Content publishing without technical knowledge.

## Phase 3 - Conversion and Automation

- Resend integration for contact form.
- Improved lead capture and email notifications.
- SEO local enhancements.
- Sitemap, metadata and social previews.

## Phase 4 - Growth

- Testimonials.
- Cloudinary or similar multimedia optimization.
- Content automation and additional integrations.
- Expanded portfolio sections and campaigns.
